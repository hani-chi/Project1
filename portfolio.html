Portfolio

Bones

When I drew this, life was overwhelming. I felt like I was being suffocated and things were closing in. I painted this around 2019. This was also a period where I studied anatomical art.

Pink

Eighth grade is when I started to get into painting; I have my art teacher to thank for that. I referenced this art and replicated it onto a ceiling tile that was later put up into the ceiling of the art room, where it still hangs today.

Unconditional

This was a piece I made for fun, roughly around the same time I painted Bones. Most of my art I like to leave up for interpretation by the audience, but to me, it shows the heart allowing the flowers to bloom even after its petals have wilted away. In a way, it represents unconditional love and support.

A Jar of Flowers

I took a fashion construction class junior year of high school, where my teacher introduced to us the art of embroidery and cross-stich. I fell in love with the craft, and it has since been a hobby of mine. A Jar of Flowers wasn't my idea, but it's what helped me start my journey in the art.
